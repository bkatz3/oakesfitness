December 2025 Hours & Scheduling

Holiday Hours & 2026 Goal-Setting Sessions

The holidays are a busy time, and we want to make sure you have everything you need to stay on track — or at least know when we're open so you can plan around us. Here's the full rundown for December and early January.

Holiday Schedule

Both studios will operate on modified hours during the holiday period:

  • Christmas Eve (Dec 24) — Both locations open 6am–12pm
  • Christmas Day (Dec 25) — Closed
  • Dec 26–30 — Regular hours
  • New Year's Eve (Dec 31) — Both locations open 6am–12pm
  • New Year's Day (Jan 1) — Closed
  • Jan 2 onwards — Full schedule resumes

If you have questions about specific session availability, reach out to your trainer directly or call the studio.

Complimentary Goal-Setting Sessions

Every January we offer complimentary goal-setting sessions to both existing clients and anyone interested in starting training. These are 30-minute conversations — no equipment, no workout — just a focused discussion about where you are, where you want to be, and what a realistic path looks like.

It's one of the most valuable things we do. The people who take time to set clear, honest goals at the start of the year consistently outperform those who don't. We've seen it year after year.

Goal-setting sessions are available January 5–16 at both locations. To book yours, contact us through the website or call either studio directly.

Happy Holidays

From Mike and the entire Oakes Fitness team — thank you for being part of this community. Wishing you and your family a restful holiday and a strong start to 2026.
