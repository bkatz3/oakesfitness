February 2026 Programs

Spring Training Programs Now Available

Spring is just around the corner, and we're kicking it off with a fresh set of training programs at both the Westford and Concord studios. Whether you're coming back after a winter slowdown or ready to push to the next level, we have something designed specifically for where you are right now.

What's New This Spring

We've put together three core program tracks launching in March:

  • Strength Foundation — A 12-week progressive strength program for clients building their base. Focuses on compound movements, proper mechanics, and steady overload.
  • Performance Edge — For clients with training experience who want to sharpen athletic performance, power output, and conditioning.
  • Mobility & Longevity — A lower-intensity program emphasizing movement quality, joint health, and functional strength. Great for clients returning from injury or managing chronic pain.

One on One and Small Group Options

All three tracks are available as one-on-one coaching sessions. We're also piloting small group formats (2–3 clients) for the Strength Foundation track at the Westford studio — a great option if you'd like the energy of a group setting with personalized attention.

How to Get Started

If you're an existing client, talk to your trainer at your next session and they'll help you identify the right track based on your current goals. If you're new to Oakes Fitness, reach out through our contact page to schedule a free consultation — we'll assess where you're starting and build a plan from there.

We're looking forward to a strong spring. Let's get to work.
