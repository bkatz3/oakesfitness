January 2026 Studio News

Westford Studio Expansion Complete

We're excited to announce that the Westford studio expansion is officially complete. After months of planning and construction, the new space is open and ready for training.

What's New

The expansion added significant square footage to the Westford location, allowing us to create dedicated zones that weren't possible before:

  • Private training rooms — Two fully enclosed rooms for one-on-one sessions, giving clients a distraction-free environment.
  • Expanded strength floor — Additional racks, barbells, and free weight stations so multiple sessions can run simultaneously without any crowding.
  • Functional training area — Open turf space for sled work, agility drills, and conditioning circuits.
  • New equipment — We've added cable machines, updated cardio equipment, and specialty bars to expand programming options.

Why This Matters

For our clients, this means more availability, better scheduling flexibility, and an improved training experience across the board. For our trainers, it means more room to design creative, effective sessions without being constrained by space.

The Westford studio has always been a great facility. This expansion takes it to a level we're genuinely proud of.

Come See It

If you haven't been in since the renovation wrapped up, come check it out. And if you've been thinking about starting training in Westford, now is a great time to reach out and schedule a free consultation.
