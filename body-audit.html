The 360° Body Audit™

Most people start a training program before they've figured out what's actually going on in their body. We fix that first. The 360° Body Audit is a 7-part evaluation that tells you exactly where you are, and builds a plan around what we find.

After 25 years of coaching, here's what we know: the assessment matters as much as the training.

Generic programs fail because they skip the individual. The 360° Body Audit removes the guesswork. We look at seven dimensions of your health and fitness, then use what we find to build something that actually fits you.

01

Posture & Alignment

We look at how you hold yourself at rest: head position, shoulder alignment, spinal curves, hip tilt. Bad posture is one of the most common hidden drivers of pain and injury after 50. Catching it early means fixing it before it becomes a real problem.

02

Movement Screen

We watch you move through the basics: squat, hinge, push, pull, carry. This shows us the compensations and weak links that standard strength testing misses. Everything we build after this gets filtered through what we see here.

03

Body Composition

Scale weight is almost useless on its own. We look at lean mass versus body fat and how that ratio has shifted over time. That gives us a real baseline to track progress against, not just a number that bounces around with water weight.

04

Cardiovascular Fitness

We get a read on your aerobic capacity. Not a grueling test, just enough to understand your recovery time, your energy levels, and how your heart handles sustained effort. That shapes how we structure sessions and how fast we ramp things up.

05

Strength Baseline

We test functional strength across key movement patterns: how much force you can produce and how well you can control it. Nothing extreme. The goal is knowing where you actually stand so the targets we set are real, not arbitrary.

06

Flexibility & Mobility

Tight hips, a stiff thoracic spine, limited ankle mobility. These things quietly cap what you can do in training and in daily life. We figure out which restrictions matter most for your goals and build them into your program from day one, not as an add-on.

07

Lifestyle & Goals

We talk about your sleep, your stress, your schedule, your history with exercise, and what you actually want out of this. A program that doesn't fit your life won't last. This conversation is how we make sure it will.
