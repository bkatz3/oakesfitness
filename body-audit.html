The 360° Body Audit™

Most people start a training program without ever figuring out what's actually going on in their body. We fix that first. The 360° Body Audit is a 7-part evaluation that tells you exactly where you are — and builds a plan around what you find.

Book Your Free Audit

25+ years of coaching taught us one thing: the diagnosis matters as much as the training.

Generic programs fail because they ignore the individual. The 360° Body Audit removes the guesswork. We look at seven dimensions of your health and fitness — then use what we find to build a program that fits your body, your life, and your goals.

Part 01

Posture & Alignment

We assess how you hold yourself at rest — head position, shoulder alignment, spinal curves, hip tilt. Postural imbalances are one of the most common hidden drivers of pain and injury, especially after 50. Finding them early means correcting them before they become a problem.

Part 02

Movement Screen

We watch you move through fundamental patterns — squat, hinge, push, pull, carry. This reveals compensations, weak links, and mobility restrictions that standard strength testing misses entirely. Everything we program after this is filtered through what we see here.

Part 03

Body Composition

Scale weight tells you almost nothing useful on its own. We look at lean mass vs. body fat, and how that ratio has shifted over time. This gives us a real baseline to measure progress against — not just a number that goes up and down with water retention.

Part 04

Cardiovascular Fitness

We gauge where your aerobic capacity sits right now — not to run you through a grueling fitness test, but to understand your recovery, your energy, and how your heart handles work. This shapes how we structure sessions and how we build intensity over time.

Part 05

Strength Baseline

We test functional strength across key movement patterns — how much force you can produce, and how well you can control it. This isn't about maxing out a barbell. It's about knowing where you actually are so we can set targets that are challenging but achievable.

Part 06

Flexibility & Mobility

Tight hips, stiff thoracic spine, limited ankle mobility — these are the things that quietly limit your training and your quality of life. We identify which restrictions matter most for your goals and address them directly in your program, not as an afterthought.

Part 07

Lifestyle & Goals

Training doesn't happen in a vacuum. We talk about your sleep, your stress, your schedule, your history with exercise, and — most importantly — what you actually want. The program we build has to fit your real life, or it won't stick. This is how we make sure it does.

Ready to see what your body is actually telling you?
