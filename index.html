Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Oakes Fitness offer?

The Westford studio offers one-on-one personal training (45-minute private sessions), Women's Total Body Conditioning group classes, and Youth Strength & Conditioning for athletes ages 11–16. The Concord studio offers one-on-one personal training.

Where is Oakes Fitness located?

Two studios: 334 Littleton Road in Westford, MA and 97a Thoreau Street in Concord, MA. Serving Bedford, Carlisle, Littleton, Acton, and surrounding communities.

How do I get started?

Request a free consultation via the contact form or call either studio directly. No commitment or payment required!

Is Oakes Fitness suitable for beginners?

Yes. Training is tailored to each individual's goals and fitness level. All fitness levels are welcome at both locations.

What makes Oakes Fitness different?

25+ years of coaching experience, fully private training spaces, and programs built around your life, not the other way around.
